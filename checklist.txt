# AUDIT: LayerZero V2 on Stellar (Soroban)
# PRIZE POOL: $101,000 USDC
# DATES: April 1 - April 14, 2026

## 1. THE WEAPONS (Soroban Constraints)
 1: 200 Read Limit per Tx (Look for: Unbounded loops, Vec iterations)
 2: TTL Storage Expiry (Look for: Missing `extend_ttl` on critical state)
 3: bytes32 vs Variable Strings (Look for: Truncation/padding in address casting)
4: No Reentrancy / Pull-Mode (Look for: Authorization bypasses in `endpoint.clear()`)

## 2. THE TARGETS (Invariants to Break)
INV-1: Endpoint owner cannot censor user messages.
INV-2: ULN / Treasury owner cannot censor via native fee manipulation.
INV-3: DVN cannot suffer a replay attack (double execution).
INV-4: Critical state (payload hashes) must never be lost.
